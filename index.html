Chaotic storage, zero reorganizing

Your cards don't stay sorted. Scrybin doesn't ask them to.

Most collection apps assume tidy, alphabetized binders. Scrybin works the way real collections do: cards land in whatever box is open, and the app keeps the map.

STEP 1

File cards wherever they land

Add a card to any bin, box, or shelf, under whatever name is on the label. Scrybin records the location of every copy.

STEP 2

Search when you need one

Look up any card by name, set, or location. Every copy you own comes back with its exact spot, in seconds.

STEP 3

Check out into decks

Move cards from bins into a deck, then check the whole deck back into any bin when you take it apart. Every move is recorded.

The full toolkit

Everything else it does

FIND

Find any card

Search by name, set, or location. See how many copies you own, which bin each one is in, and which are out in a deck.

DECKS

Track decks & checkouts

Check cards out of bins into decks and back again. A card's full movement history is always there.

SETS

Lock a bin to a set

Dedicate a bin to a single MTG set and file into it with confidence. Scrybin flags anything that doesn't belong.

IMPORT

Import your collection

Bring your cards in from a CSV, including ManaBox exports, and start finding cards the same day.

EXPORT

Export anytime

Your data walks out the door whenever you want it: native CSV or Moxfield format. No lock-in.

AUDIT

Audit your bins

Work through a bin row by row to confirm the app matches the cardboard, and catch drift before it spreads.

No account. No backend. No tracking.
